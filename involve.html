How to Get Involved

Participant

Undergraduate and graduate students from every academic program are invited to participate on design teams throughout the Lab. Applications and schedules are on our website.

Knowledge Partner

Share your experience and expertise in the form of a case study for the briefing packet. We are collecting case studies that demonstrate best practices and challenges of increasing access to sanitation.

Mentor

Practitioners, professionals, and academics are invited to attend the Lab to share their experience and expertise related to being on the ground in Myanmar, implementing sanitation projects, or business design.

Sponsor/Supporter

Monetary and in-kind donations are welcome. Your contribution can help offset the cost of food/refreshments, travel and lodging for mentors, conference supplies, and venue fees. Any level of support is appreciated.

Knowledge Partner

Please answer these questions and email them to park@cooper.edu

1. What would you say was your biggest obstacle starting a business in a foreign country?
2. Were there cultural differences?
3. What were your solutions to them?

Mentor

Please answer these questions and email them to park@cooper.edu

1. Is there a basic first step to starting a business?
2. What is the most important thing for new businesses?
3. What helps a business grow?
4. Is it more effective to focus on a certain audience and then broaden your range of targets, or have a broadly applicable approach from the beginning and then start to focus individual cases later?

Sponsor

Please answer these questions and email them to park@cooper.edu

1. Is social enterprise something that your company/group advocates or is involved in?
2. Do you want an opportunity to support students in Engineering, Art, and Architecture from the Cooper Union?
3. Do you want to provide these students with this event and allow them to interact with a real world problem?
